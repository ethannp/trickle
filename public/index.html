How to Play TRICKLE


TRICKLE is a 2-player Wordle variant, in which players are trying to guess the other player's word, while simultaneously thwarting the other player from guessing theirs.

For the meaning of colors, normal Wordle rules apply.

Both players start out by choosing an English word to have the other player guess. Let's say you choose HELLO and Player 2 chooses WORLD.

The game begins. Let's say Player 2 guesses VIDEO (for your hidden word). In normal Wordle, they would recieve that O was green and E was yellow. In TRICKLE, you get to mess with the results before feedback is sent to the other player.

Player 2 would know either one tile or no tiles have been TRICKLE'd. The player who guesses the other player's word first wins.
